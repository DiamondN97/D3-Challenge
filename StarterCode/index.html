Correlation Between Obesity and Income

Is there a correlation between the state's average median income, and the average highest body mass index?.

There appears to be a negative correlation between the median income of the state, and the average highest body mass index per person.

There are some exceptions and outliers, such as Colorado, which has the lowest average high BMI, but only above average median income.

The notable data that appear to most greatly adhere to this trend are Mississippi at one extreme, and D.C. at the other.
